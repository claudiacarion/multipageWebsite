Hi! I'm Claudia!

Frontend Developer student at Futuregames Stockholm

For as long as I can remember, I was fascinated by computers. It amazed me that with a few clicks here and there, the computer would do things I wanted it to do. So I'm not exaggerting when I say my mind was blown the first time I was introduced to HTML. What?! I typed a bunch of things on Notepad and it turned into a website?! That was the Y2K. Since then, no matter what I was studying or which field I was working in, I was always led back to computers and technology. After working in education for many years, I decided to combine my passion for creativity with my interest in technology and pursue a career as a developer. I completed School of Applied Technology Stockholm's Fullstack Javascript Bootcamp in 2022 and now I am a student in Futuregames Stockholm's Frontend Developer program.
